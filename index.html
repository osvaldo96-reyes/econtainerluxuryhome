    Architecture & Design

    This exclusive project consists of transforming a maritime container into a mini house with all the comforts and luxuries of a traditional house. It offers well-distributed spaces for circulation and the comfort of those who inhabit it. The mini houses, a project in great growth in the United States and Europe, has come to Mexico with very innovative ideas which allow:

    • To be able to relocate them at the moment you want (movable on low platforms).
    • We have designs of one level, or two levels.
    • We have one and a half or two bedroom houses.
    • We have modern and / or contemporary architectural designs.
    • They offer you the best security and little maintenance.
